About Us

Where Simple Luxury & Tranquility Meet

A search for Nature, adventure, agro learning and the healing are at the heart of YR's Nature Farm. YR's Nature Farm is a agro stay program  for those interested in a true nature , organic farming, eco and agro tourism just 30 minutes’ drive from Thane.

YR's Nature Farm unique location offers stunning and uninterrupted panoramas of the dam back water from every tent, providing all our guests with ever-changing but always dramatic and breathtaking views with abundant nature and scenic attractions.

Settle comfortably in YR's Nature Farm the premier agro stay, plan your agro explorations and transform your visit to the agro learning as an exhilarating experience.

For those who love to swim we have got a lovely outdoor swimming pool overlooking the dam back water, hills and farm.
